Our Logo

The Story Behind the Logo

We came up with the Logo by brainstorming what our content is, what we want our identity to be, and what we like to do. We settled for the Gavel in the centre as the symbolism that we review or (Judge) different kinds of food from across the globe. We settled for the Red and Orange colours because we wanted to be vibrant and in some ways stand out. After all the brainstorming and editing we ended up with the finished Logo for The Food Court!
